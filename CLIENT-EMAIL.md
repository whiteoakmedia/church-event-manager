## Email body

Hi Pastor [Name],

Over the past couple of months I've shipped twelve releases of the Church Event Manager plugin powering your events page — the most recent one (1.6.0) went live today. I wanted to give you a quick recap so you know what's new and so your team can take advantage of the improvements.

The biggest wins for you: the **Registrations page** in your dashboard is dramatically better. You can now filter by date range, by whether someone has actually checked in, and the bulk-action toolbar is in one place. Every row shows the registration type at a glance (Adult, VIP, etc.), and the CSV export now respects whatever you've filtered to — so what you see is what you download. The **Reports page** has real charts now, with one-click presets for the last 7, 30, or 90 days, plus a category breakdown. And the **Check-In screen** finally has an "Add Walk-in" button — for those Sunday mornings when someone shows up who didn't pre-register, your volunteer can add them and check them in in a single tap, no need to bounce out to the registration form.

On the reliability side, I closed a privacy issue, fixed a calendar bug that was hiding events with no end time, eliminated a race condition that could oversell capacity in rare cases, and activated a previously dormant alert that emails you when an event hits 80% and 100% full. There's also a new behind-the-scenes system that automatically reports any plugin errors back to my dashboard — so going forward, if something breaks on your site, I see it and can fix it without you needing to track me down.

I've attached a full changelog as a PDF if you or your team want to see the complete list. Let me know if you have any questions or run into anything that doesn't behave the way you'd expect.
